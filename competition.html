Judge Adjudication

At BOTS, the judging panel will be formed by 2 EXECUTION JUDGES and 2 PERFORMANCE JUDGES for a total of 4 judges per competition:

EXECUTION JUDGE - 50 points

  • Difficulty - 25 points
  • Staging - 25 points

PERFORMANCE JUDGE - 50 points

  • Cohesive - 25 points
  • Stage Presence - 25 points

For a Max Total of 200pts

Adjudicated Awards

GOLD 170 - 200 points
SILVER 140 - 169.9 points
BRONZE 70 - 139.9 points

TOP OVERALL AWARDS

At BOTS, we would recognize the top 3-5 highest Bronze, Silver, and Gold adjudication scores. As BOTS is a non-level competition, we aim to provide an accurate score per routine to provide a path of success for each participating piece.

SPECIAL AWARDS

BOTS PASS: this will be awarded to a routine that didn’t make the top 3-5 highest scores, but the judges unanimously agree that want to see them again on the closing show/dance off.

STUDIO CHAMPIONS: Top 3-5 scoring routines per Age Division/Category will advance to the Closing Show. The two top highest-scoring studios on the closing show will dance off in an “ALL STYLES” battle for a chance to become the Studio Champion of that region.
