ABOUT BOTS

Welcome to
BATTLE OF THE STUDIOS

At B.O.T.S. ...

we're all about forging a united dance community across the United States. We ignite the competitive fire within our dancers, providing a safe environment where everyone can thrive and celebrate together.

Get ready to witness electrifying performances

and connect with dancers from all walks of life. We embrace every dance style imaginable, from ballet to hip-hop, contemporary to tap, and beyond. Our stage becomes a canvas for dancers to showcase their versatility and push their boundaries.

But it's not just about the competition

it's also about the bonds we create. We believe in supporting one another, inspiring greatness, and leaving a lasting impact. Battle of the Studios isn't just an event; it's a collective journey of growth and camaraderie.

Step into our world of pulsating energy and creativity.

Workshops, masterclasses, and encounters with industry pros await you. Get ready to elevate your skills, expand your horizons, and unleash your true potential.

Join us for a mind-blowing experience that transcends dance.

Battle of the Studios is where dreams become reality, where passion unites us, and where the beat never stops.

Are you ready to dance, connect, and ignite the stage? Let the battle begin!
