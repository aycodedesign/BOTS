RULES & REGULATIONS

Registration

BOTS dancers must be registered to compete. All fees are per dancer, per routine. Registration is on a first-come, first-served basis, with final payment in full guaranteeing event participation.

Eligibilty & Restrictions

BOTS dancers must be registered to compete. All fees are per dancer, per routine. Registration is on a first-come, first-served basis, with final payment in full guaranteeing event participation.

Payments & Fees

BOTS is a free admission event. We encourage all registered dancers to invite their families and friends. Competition fees must be received before the event's early bird deadline to avoid a late payment. Competition fees must be paid in full 30 days before the event. After this, late fees may apply. Refunds are NOT available under any circumstances unless BOTS cancels an event. If a dancer cannot participate, a credit equal to the fees paid for the Competition will be given towards a future event. This credit will remain valid for up to one year. All credits will be issued back to the studio account. Dance studios may apply their additional administrative fees. [*This is very common in the industry and covers the time and expense Studio Owners incur while organizing, registering, and attending these events.]Bank Money Orders and Bank Wires must be payable in US Funds only. All returned checks will be subject to a fee equal to the amount BOTS is assessed.

Workshop Experience

The attendees' age limit is five years old. While admission to the competition is free, access to the Workshops is not. Family and friends accompanying participants can obtain an admission pass for the workshop for $5 per person.

Adjudication & Divisions

All fees are per dancer, per routine. BOTS reserves permission to use any photos and videos from the event for advertising and publicity. BOTS judges can determine that a routine may have been entered in the incorrect division/style and can move it to a more fitting division. Age is calculated as of the first of the current calendar year. Studios must purchase additional time upon registration if routines exceed the time limit. The fee is $15 per dancer, per minute.

*routines must be registered before the discount deadline by the specific city event date

Event Details

Each dancer will be issued a wristband at registration with the color indicating the division in which the dancer is competing. A separate wristband will also be issued if the dancer is also participating in the Workshop series. Entry limits will vary by event. Please look at the corresponding city page for specific entry limits for your tour city. Studio Directors are welcome to move dancers to a different age group for the Workshops if space is available.

Keep It Clean: BOTS is a family event, and inappropriate routines will be penalized. Judges may deduct 5 points from routines they deem not "family friendly," and disqualification is at the judges' discretion.

Props: Using fire, weapons, alcohol or drug paraphernalia, fog machines and hazers, liquids or messy materials, and animals is prohibited. Props must be approved by the BOTS team and set up and removed within one minute before and after the routine. If more than one minute is needed for set up and strike, a fee of $10 per minute per dancer will be added.

Stage Cleanliness: If the stage becomes littered during a routine, it should be cleaned immediately after is over by the responsible studio.
