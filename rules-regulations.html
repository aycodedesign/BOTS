RULES & REGULATIONS

Registration

BOTS dancers must be registered to compete. All fees are per dancer, per routine. Registration is on a first come, first serve basis, with final payment in-full guaranteeing event participation.

Eligibilty & Restrictions

Studio Directors, Teachers, Choreographers, and Professional Dancers are not allowed to compete. Soloists/duos/trios over the age of 18 are eligible for adjudication only. No choreography by BOTS judges, faculty, or staff for a registered dancer routine. The routine will be disqualified from the competition with NO refund of fees.

Payments & Fees

BOTS is a free admission event. We encourage all registered dancers to invite their families and friends. Competition fees must be received before the event's early bird deadline to avoid a late fee. Early bird deadline is normally 30 days prior to the event date, with exceptions noted on [hyperlink] individual event pages. Refunds are NOT available under any circumstances unless BOTS cancels an event. [asterisk w/ note at bottom for following: If a dancer is unable to participate, credit equal to the fees paid for the Workshop Experience: Studios Unite will be given towards a future event. This credit will remain valid for up to one year. All credits will be issued back to the studio account.] Dance studios may apply their own additional administrative fees. [*This is very common in the industry and is used to cover the time and expense Studio Owners incur while organizing, registering, and attending these events.] Bank Money Orders and Bank Wires must be payable in US Funds only. All returned checks will be subject to a fee equal to the amount BOTS is assessed.

Workshop Experience

Attendees' age limit is 5 years old. While admission to the competition is free, admission to the Workshop Experience: Studios Unite is NOT. Family and friends accompanying participants can obtain an admission pass for the workshop for $5 per person.

Adjudication & Divisions

All fees are per dancer, per routine. BOTS reserves permission to use any and all photos and video from the event for advertising and publicity. BOTS judges can determine that a routine may have been entered in the incorrect division/style and can move it to a more fitting division. Age is calculated as of the first of the current calendar year. Studios must purchase additional time upon registration if routines are over the time limit. The fee is $15 per dancer, per minute [*routines must be registered prior the discount deadline in accordance with the specific city event date]

Event Details

Each dancer will be issued a wristband at registration with the color indicating the division in which the dancer is competing. If the dancer is also participating in the "Studios Unite" workshop experience, a separate wristband will also be issued. Entry limits will vary by event. Please see corresponding city page for specific entry limits for your [hyperlink] tour city. Studio Directors are more than welcome to move dancers to a different level for the convention if space is available. NOTE: dancers that dance down must still audition in their originally assigned age level. These changes can be made upon registration.

Performance Considerations

Keep It Clean: BOTS is a family event, and inappropriate routines will be penalized. Judges may deduct 5 points from routines they deem not "family friendly," and disqualification is at the judges' discretion. All music must be clean, edited, and appropriate for all ages. It needs to be uploaded for pre-approval one week prior to the event. A $10 late penalty fee is charged for late music submissions. The use of fire, weapons, alcohol or drug paraphernalia, fog machines and/or hazers, liquids or messy materials, and animals is prohibited. Prop Usage: Props must be approved by the BOTS team and set up and removed within one minute before and after the routine. If more than one minute is needed for set up and strike, a fee of $10 per minute per dancer will be added. Stage Cleanliness: If the stage becomes littered during a routine, it should be cleaned immediately after the routine is finished.
